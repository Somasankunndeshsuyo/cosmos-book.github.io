Cosmos
The infographic book of space

Stuart Lowe & Chris North, 22nd October 2015, Aurum Press Ltd

Life. The Universe. Everything. The human race has always revealed an insatiable hunger to search "to infinity and beyond". Astronomers Stuart Lowe and Chris North use cutting-edge infographics to illuminate the most amazing places and objects that modern science has laid bare. Featuring innovative, inspirational and original designs, the book delves into a truly international subject and will appeal to stargazers and space enthusiasts of all ages.

You can order on Amazon.co.uk, Amazon.com, Waterstones, and Book Depository. Or support your local bookshop.

This repository accompanies the book. It contains interactive versions of selected infographics, updates, corrections, and the data/sources behind the graphics. We've also collated some of our reviews and awards. There's even a quiz!

The book is also available in 中文 (Chinese), 日本語 (Japanese), and Français (French) language translations.

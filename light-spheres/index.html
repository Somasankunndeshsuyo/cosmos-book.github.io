Light spheres

This graphic is a representation of the distance light has travelled since transmission compared to a variety of stars. The distances of the stars from Earth are to scale but the positions are not.

Visualisation based on star/planet data and light sphere data compiled for Cosmos: The Infographic Book of Space / Stuart Lowe & Chris North

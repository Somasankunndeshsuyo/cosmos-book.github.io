The local sheet of galaxies

The book contains a visualisation of the local sheet of galaxies. You can download a CSV file containing the galaxies that went into the graphic.

Each white ring represents 1 million lightyears from the Milky Way.

Based on data from McCall, MNRAS, 440, 1, pp 405-426 (2014) visualised for Cosmos: The Infographic Book of Space / Stuart Lowe & Chris North

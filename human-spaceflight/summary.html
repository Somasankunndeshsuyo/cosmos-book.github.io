For the book we collated data about everyone who has been to space* and we will aim to keep up-to-date here. You may also be interested in exploring the data, who is in space, a breakdown of astronauts per year, our human spaceflight timeline, or our map of astronaut birthplaces.

You can browse our data for each individual astronaut on Github or, if you prefer, get all the astronaut data as tab-separated values/JSON. Please let us know if you spot mistakes or, even better, fix it for yourself in the repository and submit a pull request. Data were compiled from the NASA Information Summaries Astronaut Fact Book, the NASA History Office, spacefacts.de and other sources for Cosmos: The Infographic Book of Space / Stuart Lowe & Chris North
